Dr. Harriman Saragih is a Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (UK) serving in the Master of Business Innovation program at Monash University, Indonesia. His excellence in industry engagement led to a nomination for the Vice-Chancellor’s Award for Teaching Excellence (Monash Australia) and the Dean’s Award for Industry and Community Education (Monash Business School).

A Fulbright Scholar at Cornell University (US), he advanced pedagogical and executive education expertise, including a specialization in Applied Data Science from Columbia University. He was a finalist in the Generative AI × Assessment category at Monash TransformEd (Malaysia), evidencing impact in integrating generative AI into higher education.

His innovative problem-solving is supported by IBM certifications (Design Thinking Practitioner). Prior roles include Training Analyst at CNOOC; leadership in executive education at Prasetiya Mulya (Head of Executive MBA; Associate Consultant, Executive Learning Institute), and coaching Indonesia’s team to the finals of the HSBC/HKU Business Case Competition.

Partnerships span AIBC, Alliance for Digital Health, Monash School of Pharmacy, and Monash Business School; he is affiliated with the Monash Data Futures Institute. Research interests include B2B marketing and services inclusion with A*/A publications (EJM, JSM, JBIM) using diverse methods such as SEM, machine learning, and NLP.
